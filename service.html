Our Services

Discover the ease and security of managing your transactions with our Pay in/Pay out services. Whether you're looking to make payments or receive funds, our platform offers a seamless and reliable experience for all your financial needs.

Recharge

Instantly top up your mobile, DTH, or data card with our quick and secure recharge service. Never worry about running out of balance again. We support all major providers, ensuring you stay connected.

Bill Payment

Simplify your life with our comprehensive bill payment solution. Pay your electricity, water, gas, and other utility bills effortlessly. Set up auto-pay or make one-time payments - all from one convenient platform.

Gift Voucher

Spread joy with our digital gift vouchers. Choose from a wide range of popular brands and send instant gifts to your loved ones. Perfect for last-minute presents or to show appreciation from afar.

Micro ATM

Empower local businesses with Micro ATM services. Enable cash withdrawals, balance inquiries, and fund transfers at neighborhood shops. Bring banking to every corner, enhancing financial inclusion in areas with limited banking infrastructure.

AEPS

Aadhaar Enabled Payment Services (AEPS) facilitates secure biometric-based transactions. Ideal for rural and semi-urban areas, it allows banking services using Aadhaar authentication, promoting financial inclusion and ease of transactions.

LIC Payment

Simplify your Life Insurance Corporation (LIC) premium payments with our hassle-free service. Pay your premiums on time, ensure continuous coverage, and manage your policies effortlessly without visiting the branch. Save time and stay protected.
